Why this series exists

Our families chose NYOS because we love this school deeply. Like so many of you, we care about protecting what makes it special. But over the last few years, a lot of parents — especially longtime families — have felt like something has shifted.

Programs are different. The culture has changed. Things that used to feel really intentional feel a little less clear. So instead of speculating, a few of us started asking questions — looking into timelines, leadership changes, how decisions get made, and things happening behind the scenes that most families never really hear about.

This series is just that: simple, factual parent updates. Information, context, and transparency — so families can come together and be informed. Because NYOS belongs to all of us.
